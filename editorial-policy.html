Editorial Policy

Horn Updates Horn of Africa AI-assisted summaries
Last updated: December 2025

Our Mission

Horn Updates helps readers stay informed about the Horn of Africa by publishing concise, original summaries of reporting from reputable news organizations. We aim to reduce information overload while encouraging readers to access full context on the publisher’s website.

What We Publish

  • Original summaries written by Horn Updates based on third-party reporting.
  • Headlines and metadata (source, date, country/topic tags) to help navigation.
  • Links to original publishers so readers can read the full article.

Attribution & Linking

We clearly attribute each story to its original publisher and provide a direct link to the full article. Full articles remain the property of their respective publishers.

AI Use & Human Oversight

Horn Updates uses automated tools (including AI) to assist with summarization, tagging, and formatting. We strive to ensure summaries are accurate, non-misleading, and add value beyond copying.

  • We avoid sensationalizing headlines.
  • We aim to keep summaries factual and grounded in the source report.
  • We label summaries as written by Horn Updates and link to the full source.

Corrections Policy

If you believe a summary contains an error or lacks important context, we want to fix it. Please email us with the article link and the correction request.

Contact: contact@hornupdates.com

Publisher Requests / Takedown

If you are a publisher and would like a summary corrected, updated, or removed, contact us with the URL and details of your request. We will review and respond as quickly as possible.

Editorial Independence

Horn Updates is an independent project. Our editorial choices (what to summarize, how to tag, and how to present stories) are based on relevance to the Horn of Africa and reader usefulness.

Advertising & Monetization

Advertising may appear on some pages. Ads do not influence our summaries or how we rank or tag stories. We aim to keep ads from appearing on pages without sufficient publisher content.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Editorial Policy over time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
