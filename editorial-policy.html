Last updated: January 2026

Horn Updates aims to provide clear, neutral summaries with transparent sourcing.

1) What Horn Updates is

Horn Updates is a news aggregation and summarization site focused on the Horn of Africa. We summarize articles and link back to original publishers.

2) Sources

We prioritize reputable publishers and official statements. We may exclude sources that are unreliable, deceptive, or unsafe.

3) Summaries

Summaries are AI-assisted and reviewed for clarity. They are meant to capture the main points without adding new claims. Full context remains with the original article.

4) Neutrality

We aim for a neutral tone and avoid inflammatory language. When sources disagree, we may reflect that uncertainty or present multiple perspectives.

5) Corrections

If we publish a summary with a clear error, we will correct it. To request a correction, email admin@hornupdates.com with the story link and details.

6) Updates and removals

We may update summaries for clarity or remove content when required by policy, safety, or legal reasons.

7) Transparency

We display the publisher/source and link to the original article. Ads (if shown) are separated from editorial content.
