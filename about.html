About Horn Updates

Horn Updates is an independent news aggregator focused on the Horn of Africa. We curate stories across politics, security, business, society, and culture and present concise summaries with direct links to original publishers.

Our mission

Our mission is to make it easier to follow important developments in the Horn of Africa by bringing trusted reporting into one clean, readable place. We aim to provide fast context, reduce noise, and help readers discover original reporting from reputable sources.

How it works

  • We collect headlines and article metadata from publicly available feeds and sources.
  • We generate short summaries to help you understand the main point quickly.
  • We always link to the original publisher so you can read the full article at the source.

Sources and editorial note

Horn Updates does not publish full copies of articles from other publishers. Headlines, summaries, and links are provided for informational purposes. All rights to original content belong to their respective owners.
