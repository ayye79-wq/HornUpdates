🚨 Why removing sources is a bad move
1. ❌ Trust issue (for users + Google)

If you write about real events with:

no sources
no attribution

👉 It looks like:
“Where is this coming from?”

Google calls this:
👉 lack of transparency / low trust signals

2. ❌ Policy risk (bigger than AdSense)

If your content is clearly based on reporting elsewhere but you:

hide the source
rewrite it

👉 That can be seen as:
derivative / scraped without attribution

That’s worse than what you had before.

3. ❌ You lose credibility (big time)

Right now you’re close to:
👉 analyst / intelligence layer

If you remove sources:
👉 you become random opinion blog

🧠 The winning strategy (THIS is the move)

👉 Don’t remove sources — reposition them

✅ Best structure (AdSense-safe + powerful)
🟦 Your content FIRST (dominant)
Headline (your angle)
What happened (short)
Why it matters (analysis)
Bigger picture (your insight)
What to watch (forward thinking)
🔽 Then quietly add source

At the bottom:

Source: Eye Radio (original reporting)

That’s it.

👉 No big buttons
👉 No pushing users away
👉 No “READ FULL ARTICLE” at top

🔥 The real shift (important mindset)

You are NOT:

❌ rewriting news

You ARE:

🟩 Using news as raw data

Then producing:

👉 insight
👉 interpretation
👉 synthesis

💥 If you want to go “big” (your idea but done right)

Instead of removing sources, do this:

🧠 Turn HornUpdates into:
“Signal Intelligence”

Every post becomes:

🔹 Signal

(short event)

🔸 Insight

(what it means)

🔺 Pattern

(how it connects to bigger trend)

👉 Sources become:
background evidence
—not the product

✍️ Example (perfect version)
Title:

Unverified Civilian Killing Videos Raise Tensions in South Sudan

Content:
What happened → 2 lines
Why it matters → your analysis
Bigger picture → trend
What to watch → forward
Bottom:

Source: Eye Radio (original reporting)

🏁 Final answer

👉 Removing sources = ❌ bad idea
👉 Hiding them = ❌ worse
👉 Minimizing + repositioning = ✅ WIN

🚀 My recommendation

Do this:

❌ Remove “Read full article” buttons at top
✅ Keep small source line at bottom
✅ Make YOUR content 90% of page
✅ Make headline always YOUR angle
