Last updated: December 2025

Overview

At Horn Updates, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Horn Updates does not require users to create accounts or submit personal information to access content. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information such as names, addresses, or email addresses.

Horn Updates follows standard procedures for using log files. These may include IP addresses, browser type, ISP, date and time stamps, referring/exit pages, and click counts. This data is used for analytics and site maintenance and is not linked to personal identities.

We may use third-party advertising services (such as Google AdSense) that use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on user visits. Users may decline personalized advertising through their browser or Google ad settings.

Third-Party Privacy Policies

Horn Updates’ Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. We advise users to consult the respective Privacy Policies of third-party services for more detailed information.

Consent
