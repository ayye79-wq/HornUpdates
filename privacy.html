Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Horn Updates ("we," "our," or "the site") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using hornupdates.com, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Horn Updates is an independent analysis platform. We do not require registration, login, or any personal information to access content. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information directly.

The following information may be collected automatically when you visit:

  • IP address (used for security and spam prevention)
  • Browser type, operating system, and device type
  • Pages visited, time on site, and referring URLs
  • Date and time of your visit

2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. Horn Updates uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Advertising cookies, used by Google AdSense to deliver relevant advertisements (see Section 4).
  • Analytics cookies, used by Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site, including pages visited, time on site, and navigation paths. This data is anonymous and aggregated.
  • Functional cookies, to remember your filter preferences (such as language or country selection).

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site. This may involve the use of cookies and anonymous usage data such as pages viewed, session duration, and general location (country/city level). No personally identifiable information is collected. Data is processed by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. However, some parts of the site may not function properly without cookies. You can also opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings or via the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

3. How We Use Information

Information collected automatically is used to:

  • Operate and maintain the website
  • Analyse site traffic and usage patterns to improve content and layout
  • Detect and prevent security threats or abuse
  • Display advertising (see Section 4)

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Google AdSense and Third-Party Advertising

Horn Updates uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site.

For more information on how Google uses data when you visit sites that use Google's advertising services, please visit: How Google uses information from sites that use our services.

5. Third-Party Links

Horn Updates links to external news publisher websites. These third-party sites have their own privacy policies, and we have no control over their practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external site you visit.

6. Children's Privacy

Horn Updates does not knowingly collect or solicit information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable data protection laws, including the right to access, correct, or delete data we hold about you. Because we do not collect personal information directly, most of these rights apply primarily to data held by our third-party service providers (Google, etc.). You can exercise those rights through their respective privacy controls.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
