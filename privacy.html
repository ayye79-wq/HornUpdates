Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Horn Updates collects and uses information on this website.

At Horn Updates, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy describes what information may be collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Horn Updates does not require users to create accounts or submit personal information to access content. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information such as names, addresses, or email addresses.

Log Files

Horn Updates follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files may include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. This information is used for analytics and site administration and is not linked to personally identifiable information.

Cookies and Advertising

Horn Updates may use cookies to improve user experience and measure site performance. Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on a user’s prior visits to this website or other websites. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google’s Ads Settings.

Third-Party Links

Our site contains links to external websites operated by publishers and other third parties. We do not control the content or privacy practices of those websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.
